Hi, I'm Kevin Hoang Nguyen — a dedicated licensed real estate broker with a deep understanding of the Houston metropolitan area and over 4 years of experience helping clients achieve their real estate goals.

Having lived in Alief, Sugar Land, Cypress, Richmond, and Fulshear, I bring firsthand knowledge of these dynamic communities. I've also spent significant time in Bellaire and Katy, and frequently visited Missouri City and Rosenberg, giving me valuable insights into the unique characteristics and opportunities each of these areas offers.

I've had the privilege of helping over 30 clients from diverse backgrounds find their perfect homes. Each experience has only strengthened my passion for making the home-buying and leasing process smooth and enjoyable. With a bachelor's degree in cybersecurity, I know how to leverage technology to better serve my clients — whether that's using advanced tools for property searches or ensuring secure communication and data handling.

I started my real estate career at just 21 years old, bringing youthful energy, a fresh perspective, and plenty of time to dedicate to my clients' success. My strong base of repeat clients reflects my commitment to excellent service, market expertise, and a personalized approach — along with a growing understanding of real estate investment opportunities.

When challenges arise, I lean on my trusted network of experienced professionals to make sure my clients always receive the best guidance possible.

Whether you're buying, selling, or leasing, you can count on me to work tirelessly on your behalf.

Let's make your real estate journey seamless and rewarding!

Services

What I Offer

01
🏢

Commercial Properties

Whether you're starting a business or searching for an apartment, both fall under commercial services — and I'm here to help.

For business owners, I've assisted clients in securing office space tailored to their needs, like leasing an office for an insurance agency. I'll provide a curated list of properties based on your criteria, schedule viewings, and help negotiate key terms such as monthly rate and tenant improvement (TI) allowances.

For apartment seekers, I offer referral services. I can send you a list of recommended apartments, or if you already have one in mind, simply list my name as your referral. If the property offers referral compensation, you'll receive a portion of that commission.

Looking for your next business space or apartment? Contact me today to get started!

02
🏡

Buying Assistance

Thinking about purchasing a new build? If you list me as your agent during registration, I guarantee a rebate back to you at closing. You can negotiate and sign everything directly with the builder — I don't even have to be present unless you'd like me there to assist.

With experience helping everyone from investors to first-time home buyers secure their homes, I offer more than just guidance — I provide tailored support at every step. From recommending competitive offer prices to connecting you with professionals such as trusted inspectors, I'm here to simplify your journey.

I also help you navigate negotiations to get the best possible deal and can connect you with reputable local mortgage professionals to ease your financing process.

Ready to find your perfect home? Contact me today to get started!

03
💰

Selling Your Property

I provide a thorough comparative market analysis (CMA) to recommend the optimal pricing strategy, along with insights on days on market (DOM) for similar properties in your area. You'll receive all offers presented clearly, so you stay in control of who you sell to.

I also hire professional photographers for every listing — because great photos make a huge difference, and I don't cut corners like some realtors do.

Plus, if you'd like a rebate back at closing, just ask! Contact me today to discuss your selling options.

04
🔑

Leasing Services

With over 35 lease deals successfully closed representing both landlords and tenants, I have the process down to a science to help both parties achieve their goals.

For landlords, I provide a comprehensive CMA to recommend competitive pricing and ensure your property leases quickly. I conduct thorough background and credit checks and verify all information to protect your interests. Timeliness is a priority — I work to get your property filled as fast as possible.

For tenants, I'll ensure you meet rental requirements and, if not, offer suggestions to improve your chances. I'll show you listings until you find the perfect home.

Ready to lease your property or find your next home? Contact me today to get started!
